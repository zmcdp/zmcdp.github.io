Bonuses

  • First Blood (first death in the whole game): extra 50 points
  • Solo Kill (death where only one person has them): 50 points
  • The Lynyrd Skynyrd (plane crash death): 100 points
  • Spite pick death: 50 points
  • Daily Double (two deaths in one day): 100 points
  • Birthday (celeb dies on their bday): 50 points
  • Monthly Death (dies on the 1st of the month): 50 points
  • Murder/Suicide: 50 points
